Limitations & What Genetics Cannot Tell You

  • Genetic variants indicate statistical associations, not certainties
  • Most variants have small effect sizes (1.1-1.5x risk changes)
  • Lifestyle factors often have larger effects than genetics
  • Consumer genetic tests are not clinically validated for diagnosis
  • Gene expression is modified by environment (epigenetics)
  • Many traits are influenced by hundreds of genes, not just the ones tested
  • Population frequency data may not apply equally to all ancestries
